Understanding the index

The Air Quality Index (AQI) translates complex atmospheric chemistry — particulates, ozone, nitrogen oxides, sulfur dioxide, and carbon monoxide — into a single human-legible number.

Nebula AirIndex sources data in real-time from the World Air Quality Index project, which aggregates thousands of official EPA monitoring stations globally. Your local reading is resolved automatically from your network location.

Overlay it with our light-pollution atlas, and a new narrative emerges: the cities that blaze brightest often breathe the hardest.
